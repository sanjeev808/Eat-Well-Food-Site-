Watermelon Juice

Ingredients

  • 1½ cup watermelon
  • ½ lemon juice
  • few mint / pudina
  • ¼ tsp pepper powder
  • few ice cubes

Steps

  1. firstly, in a blender take 1½ cup watermelon. make sure there are no seeds.
  2. blend to smooth making sure everything is well combined.
  3. filter the juice to get rid of the pulp.
  4. further, pour the watermelon juice into a glass and enjoy with ice cubes.
