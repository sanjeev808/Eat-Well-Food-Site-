Orange Juice

Ingredients

  • 1 Orange
  • 1 cup coconut water
  • few mint / pudina
  • few ice cubes

Steps

  1. firstly, in a blender take 1 Orange, 1 cup coconut water, few mint and few ice cubes.
  2. blend to smooth making sure everything is well combined.
  3. further, pour the orange juice into a glass and enjoy with ice cubes.
