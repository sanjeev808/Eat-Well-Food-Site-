Waffle Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 pouch: Birch Benders Organic Confetti Pancake & Waffle Mix
  • 3 tbsp: oil (of your choosing)
  • 1/2 cup: unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup: unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 tbsp : vanilla extract
  • 2 cups : confectioners' sugar, sifted
  • 2 tbsp : milk

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle iron.
  2. Make the entire bag by combining mix with 2 1/4 cups of water and 3 tablespoons of oil.
  3. Pour waffle batter onto waffle iron and cook until golden brown (time will vary by appliance).
